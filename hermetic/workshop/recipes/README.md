# Proven Recipes

> Validated combinations that reliably transmute input to gold.

---

## Recipe Quality Criteria

A recipe earns its place here when:
- [ ] Tested in real conditions (not just theory)
- [ ] Produces consistent results
- [ ] Trade-offs are documented
- [ ] Failure modes are known
- [ ] Can be reproduced
