# Quick Status Template

**Tone:** Quick, direct

---

## Template

```
Tu inversion: ${CURRENT_VALUE} USD (al ${DATE})
```

## Variables

| Variable | Source | Example |
|----------|--------|---------|
| `${CURRENT_VALUE}` | `account.current_value_usd` | 476.04 |
