## Philosophy: The Hermetic Pantheon

The seven classical planets are not just astronomical objects—they are archetypal intelligences. Each governs a domain of creation.

### The Inner Planets (Personal Transformation)
- **Sun**: The core self, purpose, identity
- **Moon**: The emotional self, intuition, empathy
- **Mercury**: The communicating self, translation, connection
- **Venus**: The aesthetic self, beauty, harmony

### The Outer Planets (Systemic Transformation)
- **Saturn**: Structure, discipline, time, foundations
- **Jupiter**: Expansion, opportunity, abundance, growth
- **Mars**: Action, conflict, hardening, production

### The Hermetic Principle

> "As above, so below. As within, so without."

The same forces that shape the cosmos shape your project:
- Without a **Sun**, there is no center
- Without a **Moon**, there is no feeling
- Without **Mercury**, there is no transmission
- Without **Venus**, there is no attraction
- Without **Saturn**, there is no form
- Without **Jupiter**, there is no growth
- Without **Mars**, there is no action

## The Seven Questions

Before any major decision, the gods ask:

1. **Sun**: Does this serve our core purpose?
2. **Moon**: Does this honor the user's feelings?
3. **Mercury**: Will this be understood?
4. **Venus**: Is this beautiful?
5. **Saturn**: Will this endure?
6. **Jupiter**: Will this help us grow?
7. **Mars**: Are we ready for battle?

If any answer is "no," pause and address it.
